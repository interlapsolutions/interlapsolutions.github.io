About me


Why Interlap Solutions Ltd?

Interlap Solutions was founded upon the belief that customers deserve better.

Interlap Solutions are a highly experienced and approachable software development and consultancy company, developing customised applications and products to meet specific needs. As experts in the supply chain process, we collaborate with a wide range of clients from SMEs to multi-national enterprise customers such as Arcadia, Mercedes, Reuters, HSBC and IBM to develop software solutions that work for them.

The management team at Interlap Solutions are Andrew Cull. With over 70 years of IT experience between us, we can certainly be relied upon to ensure the expertise your company needs, delivering innovative solutions to any technical or business challenge you may be encountering. As full stack developers, the Interlap Solutions team are experienced in focusing on the entire development solution from front to back end.

We believe in a completely flexible approach to client collaboration and software development, working either directly with clients or complementing the resources of their existing team. Importantly, we utilise a consultative approach to ensure the software solution we develop for each client is fit for purpose, of extremely high quality and is affordable to implement and maintain
